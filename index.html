About Company

Docksid 3 Construction is a Ugandan Incorporated company and locally owned. The company offers Engineering Construction and Construction Project management. We provide engineering construction services to Power distribution and transmission companies, Parastatals, oil and pipeline companies, public works agencies, municipalities, and commercial/residential real estate developers.
